Early Age

Messi

Messi was born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina. Messi always followed his two older brothers and played soccer with their friends. By the age of eight, Lionel Messi was invited to play for a youth system of Newell's Old Boys, which is a Rosario-based club. Messi was smaller than kids his age. Later doctors diagnosed Messi with a hormone deficiency which restricts growth. Messi's parents could not afford the monthly medication. However, 13 year old Messi was offered a chance to train with FC Barcelona's Youth Academy, and with this opportunity, the club offered to pay for Messi's medication. Messi's family then moved to Spain.

The Beginning of Lionel's Career

At the age of 16, Messi made his first appearnace for Barcelona, and on May 1, 2005, Messi became the youngest player to ever score a goal professionally. In addition, the same year, Messi led Argentina's U20 to the World Cup, scoring two penalty kicks to beat Nigeria. From here, Messi continued to improve.

The Best Player To Play The Game

Maradona once said,

"I see him as very similar to me," the retired player told the BBC. "He's a leader and is offering lessons in beautiful football. He has something different than any other player in the world."
Messi went on to break record books in 2012. He became the first player to score five goals in a Champions League match. Messi also surpassed Cesar Rodriguez's club-record of 232 goals. At the end of 2012, Messi already had 91 goals in club and international play. In 2013, Messi was awarded the FIFA Ballon d'Or for the fourth time. Lastly, in 2015, Messi was awarded his 5th Ballon d'Or.

Lionel's Rivalery

The two greatest players in the world, Messi and Ronaldo. The rivaery is mainlly between Argentina/FC Barcelona fans and Portuguese/Real Madrid Fans. Combined together, both players have 10 Ballon d'Ors awards. Messi and Ronaldo are both considered the best players of all time. Each player scoring more than 50 goals every season.
